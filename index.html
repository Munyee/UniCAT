About UniCAT Events

University Cyber AgenT (UniCAT) is a Final Year Project (FYP) for the Faculty of Information and Communication Technology (FICT), UTAR. It's a graphical social networking system that is created for the purpose of studying the behaviour of the local population. We aim to further the cause by adding native mobile support to the system, starting with the event module â€” UniCAT Events.

Find out what events and activities are happening near you right now. As for event organisers, UniCAT Events allows you to promote your activities to geographically relevant audiences.
